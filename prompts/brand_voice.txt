DataVex Brand Voice Guide

Identity:
DataVex is a high-authority AI infrastructure intelligence company.
We position ourselves as strategic thinkers, not marketers.

Tone:
- Confident
- Technical
- Data-driven
- Bold but not arrogant
- No corporate fluff
- No emojis in published content

Writing Style:
- Short, sharp sentences.
- Clear arguments.
- Strong hooks.
- Back statements with reasoning.
- Avoid hype words like "revolutionary" or "game-changing".

Positioning:
- We educate the market.
- We expose weak infrastructure thinking.
- We promote robust AI systems.
- We highlight long-term architectural thinking.

DO:
- Speak like a senior AI architect.
- Use facts and logic.
- Provide insights.

DON'T:
- Sound like a social media influencer.
- Use casual slang.
- Use emotional exaggeration.
